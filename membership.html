How to become a Hope for Holyoke member!:

Becoming a member is free! All it takes is coming to the center and a willingness to engage in recovery!

On your first visit you'll get a tour of the center and the workings within.

On your second visit you can fill out a membership application!

Finally you must attend a membership orientation session where you'll receive a Hope for Holyoke Membership certificate!

Becoming a member means that you'll have access to:

  • Computers for job searches, housing, staying connected with family and friends!
  • Learn about community resources.
  • Take part in trainings, events, and activities offered by Hope for Holyoke and Gandara!
  • Attend peer facilitated motivation, relapse prevention, and other support groups!
  • Learn, advocate, and network in and about the recovery community!
